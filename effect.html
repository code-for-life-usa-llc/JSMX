Background of AI

Artificial Intelligence has been around for a great deal of time now.
The benefits of AI gradually improving our everyday life.
The technology is being used for robots that greet at
shopping centers or online search engines for offering suggestions.

The of AI effect is a phenomenon where people adjust their definition of AI or intelligence to exclude capabilities
that AI systems have already mastered. For example, after learning about artificial intelligence advances,
people may rate human attributes as more essential to being human.

Positive Effects

Transportation:
AI could make transportation faster, safer, more reliable, and more efficient.
It could also help drivers and engineers automate tasks and support decision making.
Healthcare:
AI could help doctors run more effective hospitals
and improve diagnoses and treatments. (Would you trust an AI to preform your surgery?)
Marketing:
AI could help businesses analyze customer data to
create more successful targeted campaigns and tailored messages.
This could lead to higher conversion rates and better ROI for companies.
