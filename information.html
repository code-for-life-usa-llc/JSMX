How did AI started

Research began in 1956 with the Dartmouth Summer Research Project on Artificial Intelligence, organized by Dartmouth College professor John McCarthy.
The conference's goals were to develop ideas about thinking machines,
such as how to help machines use language, form concepts, solve problems, and improve themselves. It was a theoretical way to differentiate between humans and AI.

Who invented AI?

In 1955,
McCarthy coined the term "artificial intelligence" and organized a summer workshop at Dartmouth College in 1956 to explore the idea of thinking machines. The workshop, which is widely considered the founding moment of AI research,
aimed to develop ways for machines to use language, solve problems, and improve themselves.McCarthy is also credited with developing Lisp, a popular programming language used in AI research. McCarthy is often called the "father of AI"
